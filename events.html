We have organized the following activities during DALI 2016.

If you are interested in participating to one or more Afternoon activities please send us an email before Monday 21!

  • March 29 - Welcome Reception
  • March 30 - Hike to Punta Manara
  • March 31 - Visit to the Acquarium of Genova (For Families)
  • March 31 - Gala Dinner
  • April 1 - Visit to Cinque Terre

Welcome Reception

On March 29th, to celebrate the beginning of DALI 2016, we will offer participants and their families an Italian stlye "Cena Aperitivo" at the Hotel Due Mari in Sestri Levante. The event starts at 7:30 PM


Hike to Punta Manara

On March 30th we have planned a soft hike in the neighborhoods of Sestri Levante. Our target will be Punta Manara, a promontory with a great view on the Gulf of Tigullio and Sestri Levante. The hike will take place during the break between morning and afternoon sessions.




Visit to the Acquarium of Genova

On March 31th, during the morning session of DALI's Symposium day, we have organized a visit to the Acquarium of Genova.




Gala Dinner

On March 31th, after last Symposium talk, we invite DALI participants and their families to a Gala dinner at the Hotel Due Mari. The event starts at 8:30 PM


Visit to the Cinque Terre

For April 1st, the last day of DALI, we are planning a trip to the Cinque Terre, which are among the most beautiful towns on the Riviera Ligure. Everyone is invited to join us! The visit will take place during the break between morning and afternoon sessions.
